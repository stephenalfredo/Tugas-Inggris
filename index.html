What is

Implied Detail Question

Questions of this type contain the words implied, inferred, likely, or probably to let you know that the answer to the question is not directly stated.

What is

Transition Question

Transition questions ask respondents to assess whether they consider their health or functioning to have stayed the same, improved, or worsened compared with a previous time point, often a pre-intervention time point.

What is

Short Conversation

A short convo is a brief conversation. It means that the conversation is quick and doesn't last very long. It might involve exchanging a few words or discussing a single topic without much depth.

What is

Long Conversation

A long convo is a conversation that goes on for a while. It means that the people involved talk to each other for an extended period, discussing many things or going into detail about a specific topic.
